Frequently asked

What is PropParlay?

PropParlay is an information tool for sports bettors. We show futures and game odds, each team's real win chance, and AI-assisted parlay suggestions across sports.

Can I place bets in the app?

No. PropParlay does not accept wagers, hold money, or process bets. It is for research and entertainment only.

Are the picks guaranteed?

No. Our suggestions are based on live market odds and models — they are guidance, not a guaranteed outcome. Always bet responsibly and within your means.

Where does the data come from?

Odds and scores come from licensed sports-data providers (including The Odds API), and team logos/imagery from ESPN. Markets update throughout the day.
